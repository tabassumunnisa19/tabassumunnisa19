About Me

I’m Tabassum Unnisa, an engineer with a master’s in Digital Communication and experience in teaching, project coordination, and HR. After stepping away from my professional path to focus on raising my children— from babies to toddlers to curious kids—I discovered that parenting is the greatest school of all. It taught me patience, resilience, wisdom, empathy, and the art of balancing chaos with love. Now, I’m reigniting my career with a passion for data science, AI, and emerging technologies. This blog is my journey of rediscovery, where I simplify complex ideas and share them in a way that sparks curiosity. Because age is experience, and curiosity is forever.
